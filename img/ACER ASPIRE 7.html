    • Description

    Work or play, the Acer Aspire 7 Gaming Laptop is sure to be your ideal computing partner as it comes with a host of features. Boasting a large 39.62 cm (15.6) Full HD LED IPS display, this gaming laptop ensures that every visual, be it while playing games or watching a video, is crisp and vibrant. Moreover, it comes with the combination of an AMD Ryzen 5500U processor and NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1650 GPU, making it a powerful and reliable system for everyday use.

    Product Description
    Powerful Productivity

    This laptop packs a punch in terms of performance as it comes with an AMD Ryzen 5500U processor and NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1650 graphics processor. It not only gives you enough power to seamlessly tackle your work-related tasks but also keeps you entertained as you can play games, watch movies, and do more.

    Clear Visuals

    This laptop features a 39.62 cm (15.6) Full HD LED Backlit screen, with an 81.61% screen-to-body ratio, which offers clean and detailed visuals, irrespective of whether you're playing games, creating a presentation, or watching videos. Thanks to Acer Color Intelligence and Acer ExaColor, the visuals are further enhanced.

    Easy Connectivity

    This laptop comes with a dual-band Wi-Fi 6 (802.11ax) connectivity that helps offer up to three times enhanced network throughput and reduced latency by up to 75% when compared to its predecessor, the Wi-Fi 5 (802.11ac) standard.

    Large Storage

    Equipped with a 512 GB M.2 PCIe SSD and 8 GB of RAM (expandable up to 32 GB), this laptop not only helps you store a large number of files and photos but also multitask easily.

    USB Ports

    You can enjoy fast data transfer speeds on this laptop as it comes with a USB Type-C port. Along with the USB Type-C port, this laptop also comes with one USB 2.0 port and two SuperSpeed USB 5 Gbps Type-A ports (one of these can be used for offline charging). You can also connect an external monitor via the HDMI port.

    Classy Design

    This laptop features a stylish and professional design that is sure to grab the attention of people whenever you take it out of your bag. Moreover, its backlit keyboard helps you work comfortably even in dimly lit environments.
